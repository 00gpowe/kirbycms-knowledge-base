# Yes, it works! This is your knowledge base made with Kirby!

Kirby is a simple file-based cms, made by (link: https://twitter.com/bastianallgeier text: Bastian Allgeier). At (link: http://www.6wunderkinder.com text: 6Wunderkinder) we use it as a central knowledge base for any type of documentation of our company and products. We've decided to make this open-source.

It's free!

# How to install?

Installing this knowledge base is super simple!

1. Download and install Kirby ((link: https://github.com/bastianallgeier/kirbycms/zipball/master text: Direct Download))

2. Download and install the Kirby Knowledge Base ((link: https://github.com/christianreber/kirbycms-knowledge-base/zipball/master text: Direct Download))

3. Done !
